Per-LP Contract Size Overrides

Manual contract size overrides per LP account. LP lots are normalized to client-equivalent in the coverage dashboard.
How it works: Each LP may have different contract sizes for the same symbol. Contract sizes are auto-detected from MT5 positions (Manager & Terminal sources). Use this page to add manual overrides when auto-detection is unavailable (e.g. XTB/bonus LPs) or when auto-detected values are incorrect.
Formula: displayLots = rawLpLots × (lpContractSize / clientContractSize)
